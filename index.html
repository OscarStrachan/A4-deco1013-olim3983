WELCOME TO SUSF

The leading provider for University-based sport, fitness and recreation services in the Asia-Pacific region. We offer a supportive, motivating and challenging training environment for people of all ages and fitness levels.

COME IN AND MEET THE TEAM

We are only too happy to give you a tour of our facilities so you can see all of what we have to offer!

FACILITIES

We have 2 health & fitness centres, 65+ group fitness classes per week, 220+ pieces of cardio and pin-loaded resistance equipment, 16 tennis courts, 7 squash courts, a 50m 8-lane indoor heated swimming pool, 4 full size basketball courts and a 300m² indoor rock climbing centre.

CLUBS

Join a vast array of sporting and recreational clubs for men and women with well-developed juniors programs, take part in excellent courses and world class sporting events and improve your performance under the guidance of some of Australia's most accomplished coaches and sportspeople.

PROGRAMS
